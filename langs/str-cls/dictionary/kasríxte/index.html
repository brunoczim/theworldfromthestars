kasríxte — Classical Star Language Dictionary

Definition

kasríxte (inflected for 2nd-person topical inanimate plural)
  1. Question pronoun used to refer to things in general; what.
  2. Relative pronoun used to refer to things in general; that.

Romanization

kasríxte

Pronunciation

  • Some Late CSL Accents
